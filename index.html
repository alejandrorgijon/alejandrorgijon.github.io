About me

¡Hola! I'm an environmental microbiologist who aims to understand the ecology and evolution of Archaea and Bacteria by using metagenomics, environmental metadata and other different tools.

- Since February 2025, I am a postdoctoral researcher at the National Museum of Natural History (MNCN-CSIC) in Madrid, Spain.

In this website you can find a bit of my background and research!
